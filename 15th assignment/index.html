List of Famous Historical Battles

  • Battle of Panipat

    Date: April 21, 1526

    Description: The Battle of Panipat was a significant clash between the forces of the Mughal Empire, led by Emperor Babur, and the Lodi dynasty of the Delhi Sultanate. The battle resulted in a decisive victory for Babur, establishing the Mughal Empire in the Indian subcontinent.

  • Battle of Buxar

    Date: October 22, 1764

    Description: The Battle of Buxar was fought between the East India Company and the combined forces of Mir Qasim, the Nawab of Bengal, Shuja-ud-Daulah, the Nawab of Awadh, and the Mughal Emperor Shah Alam II. The battle marked a significant turning point in British expansion in India.

  • Battle of Plassey

    Date: June 23, 1757

    Description: The Battle of Plassey was a pivotal engagement in the early years of British rule in India. It was fought between the forces of the British East India Company, led by Robert Clive, and the Nawab of Bengal, Siraj ud-Daulah. The British victory established their control over Bengal and laid the foundation for British dominance in India.

  • Battle of Hydaspes

    Date: 326 BC

    Description: The Battle of Hydaspes was fought between the Macedonian army of Alexander the Great and the Indian king Porus. The battle took place on the banks of the Hydaspes River (now known as the Jhelum River) in present-day Pakistan. Despite a valiant resistance by Porus, Alexander emerged victorious, further expanding his empire into the Indian subcontinent.

  • Battle of Kalinga

    Date: 262 BC

    Description: The Battle of Kalinga was a major conflict between the Maurya Empire, led by Emperor Ashoka, and the Kingdom of Kalinga (in present-day Odisha, India). The battle resulted in a decisive victory for the Maurya Empire but had a profound impact on Ashoka, leading to his conversion to Buddhism and the pursuit of non-violence.
