List of Movies

  • Avengers: Endgame

    Release Year: 2019

    Summary: After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, the universe is in ruins. The remaining Avengers must assemble once again to undo Thanos' actions and restore order to the universe.

  • Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge

    Release Year: 1995

    Summary: Raj and Simran meet during a trip across Europe and fall in love. However, Simran's traditional father opposes their relationship, leading to a series of challenges and a fight to win their love.

  • Interstellar

    Release Year: 2014

    Summary: In a future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable, a group of explorers embarks on a space mission to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The journey tests their endurance, as they encounter time dilation and the mysteries of the universe.

  • Baahubali: The Beginning

    Release Year: 2015

    Summary: Raised in a remote village, Shivudu learns about his true heritage and embarks on a quest to reclaim the kingdom of Mahishmati from the ruthless Bhallaladeva. The film is filled with epic battles, political intrigue, and grand visual spectacles.

  • Black Panther

    Release Year: 2018

    Summary: T'Challa, the newly crowned king of Wakanda, must defend his nation and the world from a powerful enemy. With the help of his loyal allies and advanced technology, he embraces his role as the Black Panther and protector of Wakanda.

  • Vikram Vedha

    Release Year: 2017

    Summary: A notorious gangster, Vedha, challenges a righteous police officer, Vikram, with his intriguing tales. As Vikram tries to solve the mysteries, he is faced with moral dilemmas and the blurred lines between good and evil.
